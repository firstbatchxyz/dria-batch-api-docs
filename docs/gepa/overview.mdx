title: Overview
---

GEPA (Genetic-Pareto) is Dria's automated prompt evolution engine. You provide a seed prompt, an evaluation dataset, and a budget (how many inference calls you are willing to spend). GEPA repeatedly mutates the prompt, scores the variants, and keeps the best ideas by balancing performance and diversity.

## Why GEPA

- **Automated prompt tuning** that extracts structure from your dataset instead of manual trial-and-error.
- **Sample efficient** mutations that reach strong prompts in tens of calls, not hundreds.
- **Observable progress** through lineage tracking, scores, and evaluator outputs.
- **Model agnostic** &mdash; use the same models you already rely on in production.

## When to Use It

- Customer support or sales templates that need higher clarity and personalization.
- Agent instructions for RAG or workflow automation.
- Scoring rubrics for evaluations.
- Security or compliance review flows where missing steps are costly.

GEPA exposes REST endpoints (documented in the autogenerated **GEPA API Reference**) and Python-friendly JSON. The rest of this section covers how to craft payloads, monitor executions, and interpret results.
